About Us

Ananda Marga Mission in Kenya is a charitable organization ,dedicated to serving the less fortunate in the society through various programmes search as, food distribution to over 1500 mothers every Sunday between 8am-9am,feeding programme of more than 1500 children every Sunday between 1pm -2 pm ,other programme include health care provided through Amurt hospital across the country ,education through Ananda Marga Academy mission school cross the country and shelter to orphaned children through the AnandA Marga children's home.

Food
Programme

Feeding and distribution of food distribution to vulnerable members of the community. This is done every Sunday at the Ananda Marga Mission headquarters.

  • Every Sunday food distribution to 3000 people
  • Feeding Programme for 1000 children every Sunday afternoon

Childrens
Home

The Mission Provides homes for homeless children.

  • Amanda Marga Mission Childrens Home in Mombasa: 24 children
  • Amanda Marga Mission Childrens Home in Nairobi: 20 children

Mission
School

Under this programme, Amanda Marga Mission provides free access to education to thousands of young children all over the country. The students also get to enjoy free food provided by the Mission. This programme also involves provision of education of people living with disabilities.

Health
Programme

Under this programme, the mission provide free healthcare to vulnerable members of the community. Special attention is given to children living with HIV/AIDs.
